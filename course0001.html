Course Overview

This extensive course is designed to provide a thorough understanding of product management. You'll start with the fundamentals of product strategy and market research, and progress to creating and managing product roadmaps. Throughout the course, you'll learn to balance customer needs with business objectives, prioritize features, and lead cross-functional teams.

By the end of this course, you'll have the skills to define and execute product strategies, manage the product lifecycle, and drive product success from concept to launch. Ideal for current or aspiring product managers, entrepreneurs, and professionals looking to deepen their knowledge of product management.
